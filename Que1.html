Meta Tag Explaination

meta name="description" content="...":

Purpose: Provides a brief summary of the webpageâ€™s content. This description is often used by search engines in search results.

Importance: Improves search engine optimization (SEO) and can influence whether users click on your site from search engine results pages (SERPs).

meta name="keywords" content="...":

Purpose: Lists relevant keywords that describe the content of the webpage. These keywords help search engines understand the main topics of the page.

Importance: Although not as influential in modern SEO as it once was, it can still play a role in search engines' understanding of your content.

meta name="author" content="...":

Purpose: Specifies the author of the document.

Importance: Although not as influential in modern SEO as it once was, it can still play a role in search engines' understanding of your content.
